About Me

Hello! I'm Tanishka Jain, a passionate first-year IT student at JECRC College with an unwavering love for photography and videography. My journey into the world of visual storytelling began with curiosity and has evolved into a deep passion for capturing the beauty in everyday moments.

I believe that every frame tells a story, and every shot has the potential to evoke emotions and preserve memories. Through my lens, I aim to explore different perspectives, experiment with creative compositions, and develop my technical skills in both photography and videography.

Joining this club would be an incredible opportunity for me to learn from experienced photographers, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and grow as a visual artist. I'm eager to contribute to the club's creative energy while expanding my own horizons in this beautiful art form.
